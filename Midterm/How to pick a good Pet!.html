Learn About the Breed

  • Are you an active person or more laid-back?

  • Do you have children or plan to have children?

  • How long are you away from home each day?

  • A dog’s size includes both its height and weight. Families living in a small apartment or house should consider a smaller or bigger dog.

After you get an idea of what breed of dog you may be looking for, learn everything you can about that type of dog. By purchasing a puppy from a responsible breeder, you’ll have to anticipate the temperament, size, and grooming needs. Although each dog is different, this information can be valuable in making the best determination if a breed is right for your lifestyle and needs.
